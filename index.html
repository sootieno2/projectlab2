Protecting local habitats

Small actions add up. This site collects simple, accessible steps you can take in your community.

Why it matters

The world's ecosystems provide food, water, and climate regulation. Even small local efforts help maintain biodiversity.

Latest community count: September 14, 2025.

Practical actions

  1. Reduce single-use plastics.
  2. Plant native trees and shrubs.
  3. Join or organise a local cleanup.

Tip

Use a refillable bottle instead of buying bottled water.
